Home alone sundae

Ingredients

  • Neapolitan ice cream
  • 12 regular size marshmallows
  • 8-10 maraschino cherries
  • Canned whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Soften the ice cream by microwaving for 30 sec.
  2. Scoop the entire tub of ice cream into the bowl.
  3. Place the marshamllows around the ice cream.
  4. Drizzle the chocolate sauce over the sundae.
  5. Place swirls of whipped cream around the ice cream and top with cherries.
